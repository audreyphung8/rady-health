Risk Profiling Unvaccinated Children for RSV in Orange County
Description:
This is a capstone project for UC Irvine students that analyzes RSV incidence and nirsevimab immunization rates across Orange County. The project identifies trends in neighborhoods and demographic groups with high RSV positivity rates. To support more efficient collaboration between clinicians and biostatisticians, the team proposed a query-based generative AI agent that enables clinicians to interact directly with RSV surveillance data, increasing the flexibility of cross-sectional research.
